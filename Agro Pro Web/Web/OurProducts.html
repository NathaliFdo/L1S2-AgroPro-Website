3.Vertical Garden

A vertical garden is a technique used to grow plants on a vertically suspended panel by using hydroponics. These unique structures can either be freestanding or attached to a wall.
Vertical gardens have been used since ancient civilizations; many modern vertical gardens can last for decades and give a pop of nature into modern-day business. Vertical gardens are a wonderful alternative to potted plants in the office space. While potted plants have the advantage of being placed anywhere, they can take up space and require lots of maintenance.
However, with vertical gardens, there is only one large panel to maintain, and it will provide a lush pop of color to any professional environment. Due to urbanization majority of the new generation choose to live in sky-rising apartments.
In these apartments, the land is not available even to grow flower plants to beautify these places.
In such places, the vertical garden cube is the ideal method to fulfill their desire.
This cube could be hung on walls and flowers or vegetables could be grown on these cubes. This cube requires limited space and the requirement is also covered with less cost.
